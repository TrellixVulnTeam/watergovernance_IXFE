How do we help control?

The system would detect if too much amount of water is flowing through the sensor for the prescribed amount of time. It can immediately warn the user by a mobile notification to turn off the tap. Citizens can also get daily water consumption updates, to ensure that they haven't crossed the consumption limit.

Why are we needed?

Our method is fairly cost effective. Moreover, we plan to give credits if the water consumption is below the threshold, which can be used in various government incentives. This will motivate more and more people to set goals to save water, by installing harvesting facilities and similar techniques.

How do we exactly work?

The huge amount of data generated from so many smart water meters on the database is very suitable for prediction algorithm using machine learning. Over time the prediction results would become more accurate and can be used to predict how much water storage capacity should be expanded. This would help in aloting budgets for construction projects for future planning.
